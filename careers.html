Join North State Senior Solutions

We're building something meaningful in the North State region — and we're looking for people who share our commitment to families navigating one of life's hardest decisions.

North State Senior Solutions is a growing, family-owned senior living placement service based in Northern California. We help families find the right senior living community for their loved one — with honest advice, personal guidance, and no hidden agenda.

We are in the early stages of building our team and are interested in connecting with people who are passionate about eldercare, community service, and doing right by families.

Roles We're Building Toward

Senior Living Advisor

Guide families through the community search process — from initial consultation through move-in day. Ideal background: social work, healthcare, eldercare, or a personal connection to this work. Part-time and full-time opportunities possible.

Community Relations Manager

Build and maintain relationships with senior living communities across the region. Conduct facility visits, manage referral agreements, and represent North State Senior Solutions at local healthcare and eldercare events.

Administrative Coordinator

Support our placement workflow — scheduling consultations, maintaining our CRM, managing intake follow-ups, and coordinating with families and communities. A detail-oriented, compassionate people person.

Healthcare Outreach

Build relationships with hospital social workers, discharge planners, elder law attorneys, and other referral sources across Shasta and Butte counties. Ideal for someone with existing relationships in the North State healthcare community.

What We Look For

  • Genuine care for older adults and their families
  • Honest, straightforward communicator — in person and in writing
  • Reliable follow-through — families in crisis need people who do what they say they will
  • Comfortable with ambiguity in an early-stage company
  • Knowledge of the North State healthcare or eldercare landscape is a strong plus

We are a startup. Roles may be part-time, contract, or commission-based initially. We are transparent about this. If you're looking for meaningful work in a growing field — and you're willing to help build something — we'd love to talk.

To express interest or ask questions, reach out at northstateseniorsolutions@outlook.com with a brief introduction about yourself and why this work appeals to you. No formal application required at this stage.
