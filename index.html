The Problem We Solve

This decision is hard enough
without the wrong kind of help.

Most families are navigating this for the first time, under enormous pressure.

A parent just got a diagnosis. The hospital is calling about discharge. You have two weeks to figure something out that you've never done before — while grieving, working, and managing a household.

The big national referral services flood your inbox with facilities and follow-up calls. Their recommendations reflect who pays them the most, not who fits your family best. A 2024 Senate inquiry found exactly that — and investigative reporting confirmed it.

We work differently. We visit every community we recommend. We're honest about what we don't know. And we stay with you through the whole process — not just until we make the introduction.

"I started North State Senior Solutions to be a trusted guide for families navigating senior living options for their loved ones. These decisions can feel overwhelming, especially when families are unsure where to begin. My goal is to bring clarity, compassion, and local knowledge to the search — helping every family find a community where their loved one can feel safe, supported, and connected."

— Luke, Founder of North State Senior Solutions

The Process

Three steps to clarity.

Most families go from overwhelmed to confident in 2–3 weeks. Here's how we get there together.

1

Tell us about your situation

Complete a brief assessment or call us directly. We ask about care needs, location, budget, and timeline — and we actually listen. No hard sell, no immediate flood of facility calls to your phone.

2

We build your shortlist

We curate 3–5 communities that genuinely fit your family's situation — not just everyone in our network. We share honest notes on each, including concerns. Then we help you schedule tours.

3

We guide you through the decision

We're with you through tours, questions, the residency agreement, and move-in day. And we follow up afterward — because a placement that goes well matters to us.

What We Help With

Every stage of the journey.

We help families find the right setting for where their loved one is right now — and plan for what might come next.

Independent Living

For active seniors who want community, amenities, and freedom from home maintenance — without daily care services.

Assisted Living

Communities offering help with daily activities — meals, medication management, bathing — while preserving as much independence as possible.

Memory Care

Specialized communities for seniors with Alzheimer's, dementia, and other cognitive decline. Safety and dignity, together.

Continuing Care (CCRC)

Communities offering a full continuum — from independent living through skilled nursing — so couples can stay together as needs change.
