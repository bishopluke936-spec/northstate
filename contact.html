Frequently Asked Questions

Is your service really free?

Yes — completely free to families. We receive a referral fee from senior living communities when a resident we referred moves in. This does not change what you pay the community. We disclose every referral relationship we have — you can read our full fee disclosure here.

How quickly can you help?

If your situation is urgent — hospital discharge, immediate safety concern — email us and mark it urgent. We prioritize time-sensitive situations and will respond the same day.

Do you only work with communities in your network?

No. If the right community for your family is one we don't have a referral agreement with, we'll tell you — and we'll help you reach out to them directly. We work for your family, not for the communities.

What areas do you serve?

We serve families across Shasta County (Redding, Anderson), Tehama County (Red Bluff), and Butte County (Chico, Oroville, Paradise). We have personally visited communities across all of these areas.
