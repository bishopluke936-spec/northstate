We Started This Business Because Families Deserved Better.

North State Senior Solutions is a family-owned senior living placement service built around one principle: families deserve honest, unbiased guidance when choosing a place for their loved one to call home.

We started North State Senior Solutions after seeing firsthand how difficult and disorienting this process can be. The big national referral services send your information to dozens of communities the moment you search, triggering floods of sales calls. Their recommendations are driven by who pays them the most — not who is the best fit for your family.

We built something different. We work with families the way we'd want someone to work with our own.

How We're Different

We visit every community we recommend

Every community in our network has been visited in person. We've met the staff, walked the halls, and assessed the culture before we ever recommend it to a family.

We protect your information

We don't send blanket referrals. Your contact information stays private until you've chosen a specific community and given us permission to make the introduction.

One advisor, start to finish

You work with the same person from your first consultation through move-in day. No handoffs to a call center. No disappearing after the introduction.

Transparent about how we're paid

We receive a referral fee from communities when a resident moves in. We tell you which communities we have agreements with — and which we don't. That information is yours.

Our Service Area

We serve families across the North State region of California, with deep knowledge of communities in:

  • Shasta County — Redding, Anderson, and surrounding areas
  • Tehama County — Red Bluff and Corning
  • Butte County — Chico, Oroville, and Paradise

Our database includes over 40 verified senior living communities across this region — from large campus communities to small residential care homes — spanning independent living, assisted living, memory care, and continuing care options.

Our Commitment to Families

"We never recommend a community we haven't visited. We protect your information until you're ready. We follow up after move-in — because your family still matters to us when the paperwork is done."

Our service is always free to families. We are paid by communities only when a placement occurs — and we disclose every one of those relationships up front. You can read our full fee disclosure here.
