1. Who We Are

North State Senior Solutions is a senior living placement service based in Northern California. We help families find assisted living, memory care, independent living, and other senior housing options. We operate this website at northstateseniorsolutions.com.

We are not a healthcare provider, insurance company, or financial institution. We are a placement advisory service. References in this policy to "we," "us," or "our" refer to North State Senior Solutions.

Contact: northstateseniorsolutions@outlook.com

2. What Information We Collect

We collect information in two ways: information you give us directly through our forms, and limited information collected automatically when you visit our site.

Information you provide directly

When you complete the "Connect with an Advisor" form or the "Personalized Care Assessment," you may provide:

  • Your name, phone number, and email address
  • Your relationship to the person seeking care
  • The senior's name, age, and current location
  • Care needs, medical conditions, diagnoses, and mobility status
  • Location preferences, community preferences, and lifestyle details
  • Budget range and funding sources
  • Timeline and urgency information
  • Any additional information you choose to share in open-text fields

Completing our assessment involves sharing health-related information about a senior. We treat this information with the highest level of care and discretion.

Information collected automatically

When you visit our website, we collect basic technical data including your browser type, device type, approximate location (city level), and pages viewed. This is collected through Microsoft Clarity, a visitor analytics tool. We do not collect your IP address in identifiable form.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to help your family find appropriate senior living options. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Understand the care needs and preferences of the senior you are searching for
  • Match your family with senior living communities that fit those needs
  • Contact you to discuss your options and answer your questions
  • Send you match results and follow-up information by email
  • Improve how we serve future families by understanding what works and what doesn't

We do not use your information for advertising, marketing to third parties, or any purpose unrelated to helping your family directly.

4. How We Protect Your Information

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss:

  • Encrypted transmission. Our website uses HTTPS, which encrypts all data between your browser and our servers. Form submissions travel over this encrypted connection.
  • No public database. We do not store your information in a publicly accessible database. Form submissions are received via Netlify's secure form handling service and delivered to our private business email.
  • Limited access. Your information is accessed only by North State Senior Solutions staff involved in your placement search.
  • No unnecessary retention. We do not retain your information beyond what is needed to assist your family.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we use commercially reasonable protections, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Who We Share Your Information With

We do not sell your personal information. Ever. We do not share your information with senior living communities or any other third party without your explicit permission.

When you are ready to connect with a specific community — and you tell us you are ready — we will make a warm introduction on your behalf. At that point, we share only the information relevant to that introduction, and only with your knowledge.

Service providers

We use a small number of trusted third-party services to operate this website. Each is bound by its own privacy and security standards:

  • Netlify — hosts our website and processes form submissions securely
  • Microsoft Clarity — provides website analytics (no personally identifiable information is shared)
  • EmailJS — delivers automated emails such as your match results (your email address is used only to send that specific email)

We do not share your information with any other parties.

Legal requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to a valid legal request from a government authority.

6. How Long We Keep Your Information

We retain your intake and assessment information for as long as is necessary to complete your placement search, plus a reasonable period afterward in case you return to us with additional needs. If you ask us to delete your information at any time, we will do so promptly.

7. Analytics and Tracking

We use Microsoft Clarity to understand how visitors use our website. Clarity collects information such as mouse movements, clicks, and scrolling behavior in aggregated and anonymized form. This helps us improve the site experience.

Microsoft Clarity does not collect your name, email address, or any information you enter into our forms. You can learn more about Microsoft Clarity's data practices at privacy.microsoft.com.

We do not use Google Analytics, advertising pixels, or any third-party advertising tracking on this site.

8. Your California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) gives you specific rights regarding your personal information. We honor all of these rights.

Right to Know

You can ask us what personal information we have collected about you, where it came from, how we use it, and who we share it with.

Right to Delete

You can ask us to delete the personal information we have collected about you. We will honor this request promptly and completely.

Right to Opt Out

You have the right to opt out of the sale of your personal information. We do not sell personal information, so this right is already honored by our practices.

Right to Non-Discrimination

We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your privacy rights. Our service is available to you regardless.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at northstateseniorsolutions@outlook.com. We will respond within 45 days as required by California law.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for adults who are searching for senior living options for themselves or a family member. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If you believe a minor has submitted information through our site, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our site after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, want to exercise your privacy rights, or have concerns about how your information is handled, please reach out. We take these questions seriously and respond promptly.

Questions about your privacy?
